### Creating Workspace

In your Requestly app, you can switch your workspace from the side bar. By default, you are in your private workspace.

1. Go to [Requestly Dashboard](https://app.requestly.io/rules/my-rules).

2) Click on the drop down next to **Private \</em**.

3. Go to **Create New Workspace**.

4) Give a name to your Workspace and click **Create New Workspace**.

### **Add your teammates**

Click on the **+Add Member** to add more teammates to your team workspace.
