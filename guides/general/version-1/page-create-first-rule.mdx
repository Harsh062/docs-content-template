Rules in Requestly helps you modify a specific network requests or responses. To create your first rule:

1. Go to [Requestly Dashboard](https://app.requestly.io/rules/create).

2) Select which type of rule you want to create. Learn about different types of rules from [here](https://developers.requestly.com/http-rules/overview/).

<Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-36aebfa3-46cd-4875-915f-1645a2d1b432-ss-2_17png/public" align="left" />

3. Click on **Create Rules**

4) Fill in the details of the rule and click **Create Rule**
