## Managing workspace details

### Delete a workspace

Workspaces can be deleted using the `Delete Workspace` button in the workspace settings

## Managing members

### Adding members

Admins can add members by sharing a public invite link.

Members can add member emails by clicking on the `Invite People` button and entering the emails of the new members

<Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-a772ee8f-6537-40cf-b7af-fa2d6b92c3f6-ss-2_44png/public" align="left" />

 

An invite will be sent to the emails that you enter here

<Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-prod-files-secures3us-west-2amazonawscom-8e04dd23-74cd-4c44-8158-4238eac359d1-d8000709-c69c-473b-8fe8-18af1a57480a-ss-2_45png/public" align="left" />

### Removing members

Admins can remove members from a workspace by selecting the `Remove from Workspace` option in the dropdown from the members list.

<Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-s3-us-west-2amazonawscom-securenotion-staticcom-3f9d14e6-567c-4745-8fda-4a4b5b337b1e-remove_memberswebp/public" align="left" />

In case of members who have been invited but not yet added to the workspace, you can revoke their invites using the same dropdown.
