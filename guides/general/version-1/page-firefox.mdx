Requestly can be directly installed on firefox by visiting our [home page](https://requestly.io/).

To install the Firefox add-on:

1. Go to [Requestly](https://requestly.io/) home page.

2) Click **Download** for Firefox.

3. Click **Continue to Installation**.

   1. <Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-s3-us-west-2amazonawscom-securenotion-staticcom-4dbc13cd-a964-4fcd-8874-ce3c9e449da2-untitledwebp/public" align="left" />

4) Click **Add**.

   1. <Image src="https://imagedelivery.net/IEMzXmjRvW0g933AN5ejrA/wwwnotionso-image-s3-us-west-2amazonawscom-securenotion-staticcom-0df71a9a-b8f6-4dab-bf5d-82f875612a84-untitledwebp/public" align="left" />

5. Your extension will be installed 🎉.
