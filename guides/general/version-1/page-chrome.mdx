Requestly can be installed directly on your browser by visiting our [home page](http://requestly.io/) or the [Chrome web store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/redirect-url-modify-heade/mdnleldcmiljblolnjhpnblkcekpdkpa).

To install the extension:

1. Go to the [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/redirect-url-modify-heade/mdnleldcmiljblolnjhpnblkcekpdkpa).

2) Click **Add to Chrome**, then click **Add Extension**.
