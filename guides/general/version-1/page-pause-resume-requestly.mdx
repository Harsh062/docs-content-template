To prevent Requestly rules from intercepting and modifying the requests of your websites, you can pause the rules temporarily. It will be handy when you want to test the original behaviour of your website without affecting your Requestly rules.

#### Pause Requestly:

1. Click the Requestly icon on the browser's toolbar.

2) Click on the toggle button which says **Requestly running**.

#### Resume Requestly

1. Click the Requestly icon on the browser's toolbar.

2) Click on the toggle button which says **Requestly paused**.

3. Refresh the page to start using Requestly again.
